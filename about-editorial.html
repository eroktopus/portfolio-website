About Me

I am a short form video editor primarily based in New York City, with a career spanning over two decades. My expertise lies in the advertising industry, where I have had the privilege of working with numerous top-tier agencies and esteemed clients. My creative approach and collaborative nature have been pivotal in delivering compelling and impactful video content that resonates with audiences.

I am passionate about my craft and thrive on the excitement of new challenges. Currently, I am available and eager to contribute to fun and innovative projects, bringing my experience and enthusiasm to every endeavor.
